## Overview

SoTrusty is a mobile-first platform we built to empower local businesses to launch and manage modern, commission-free online stores in minutes, using high-quality media, QR sharing, and diverse payment options.

## Key Features

- **Quick Store Setup**: Launch a full-featured online shop within minutes on any device—no coding or app required.
- **Rich Product Showcase**: Upload and sell products with videos, photos, and Web Stories to create engaging visual storefronts.
- **Flexible Payments**: Accept modern payment options (Apple Pay, Google Pay, credit cards) and manual methods (cash), plus generate QR codes and shareable links.
- **Order & Inventory Management**: Built-in system for tracking orders, confirming payments, managing inventory, and interacting with customers.
- **Marketing Tools**: Generate QR codes and promotional links for social-sharing, with optional Facebook and Instagram catalog integration.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: For fast, server-rendered React applications.
- **Figma**: For creating and managing design assets and prototypes.
- **Styled-Components**: For styling React components with a modular, themable approach.
- **Storybook**: For building an interactive, documented component library.

## Challenging Problems & Resolutions

- **Balancing Simplicity with Functionality**:
Challenge: Providing a powerful yet intuitive platform to non-technical local businesses without overwhelming users.
Resolution: Designed a clean, wizard-style interface with drag-and-drop content blocks, preset themes, and context-aware guidance—letting users configure shops in under five minutes.

- **Ensuring Stable Mobile Performance**:
Challenge: Delivering fast, crash-free experiences on mobile devices faced connection instability for some users .
Resolution:Implemented offline caching, optimized image/video loading, and added retry logic on network errors, reducing session drops and improving reliability.
